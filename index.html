01. about me

A bit about me

Hello! I'm Nawab, a frontend developer who fell in love with building things on the web back in college. After completing my BTech in Computer Science from Darbhanga College of Engineering, I spent time deepening my fundamentals through Coding Ninjas certifications before joining Appsysco Technologies as a Web Developer.

At Appsysco, I worked as part of a team building Shopify e-commerce stores and WordPress websites for international clients. I shipped 4 live client projects and developed a strong sense of how to translate design into functional, responsive interfaces.

Now I'm pursuing my Master's in Artificial Intelligence at National Kaohsiung Normal University in Taiwan. My coursework covers Data Science, Data Analysis, EDA (Exploratory Data Analysis), Python with Pandas and NumPy, and Machine Learning fundamentals — building hands-on practice through guided projects on GitHub.

When I’m not coding, I enjoy exploring Taiwan, learning Mandarin (slowly!), watching cricket, and cooking.
