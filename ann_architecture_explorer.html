Each card is one step in the pipeline. Cool cards on the left form the feature-extraction model; warm cards on the right form the MLP learning model.

Feature extraction model

Converts high-dimensional, unstructured input (an image, a sequence) into a compact feature vector — automatically, with no manual feature engineering.

MLP learning model

Fully-connected layers take that feature vector and learn the supervised mapping to the output, usually ending in a softmax for classification.

Trained end-to-end with backpropagation · CNN = spatial · RNN/LSTM = sequential
